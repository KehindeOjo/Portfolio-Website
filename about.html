About Me

The Analyst
Behind the Numbers

I see numbers as more than figures. They tell the story of how a business is performing, and my job is turning those stories into decisions.

I'm Kehinde Ojo, a Data Analyst with a strong background in financial analysis, business intelligence, and process automation. I hold a B.Sc in Accounting from the University of Lagos and work with Excel, Power BI, SQL, Python, and Google Apps Script to explore data, build dashboards, automate workflows, and generate insights that help organizations move with clarity.

I focus on three core areas:

Financial Analysis Modeling, forecasting, reporting, and cost optimization
Data Analytics KPI tracking, visualization, and insight generation using Python, SQL, Excel, and Power BI
Automation & Systems Building workflows that reduce manual effort and improve operational efficiency

Beyond my day-to-day work, I have delivered data projects spanning HR attrition analysis, profitability and cost modeling, AI adoption trends, and estate payment tracking. Each one was built around a business question and answered with structured analysis, Python EDA, and visual dashboards in Power BI. These projects reflect how I approach analytics: start with the problem, follow the data, and communicate findings in a way that drives action.

I am currently expanding into AI automation, exploring how AI tools can be layered onto existing workflows to further reduce manual effort and improve decision speed.

This work has delivered:

  • ₦5M+ recovered through financial audits
  • 30% improvement in operational efficiency through automation
  • 40% reduction in manual reporting effort
  • 25% faster reporting cycles using dashboard systems

I'm actively building toward more dedicated Data Analytics and Business Intelligence roles, and I'm at my best when the work sits at the intersection of finance, data, process automation, and operational insight.
