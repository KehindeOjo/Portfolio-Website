About Me

Data is more than just numbers. It reveals the story behind every smart business decision. I thrive on uncovering insights that lead to smarter strategies and measurable impact.

I’m Kehinde Ojo, a Data and Financial Analyst based in Lagos, Nigeria, with a strong foundation in finance and accounting. I specialize in turning raw data into actionable insights using tools like Excel, SQL, Power BI, Python (Jupyter), and QuickBooks to bridge the gap between finance, data, and technology.

I help organizations uncover trends and performance gaps, Monitor KPIs and streamline reporting, and Make confident, data-driven decisions.

My expertise spans financial modeling, data visualization, and business intelligence, enabling me to solve complex business challenges with clear, data-led solutions. From analyzing financial transactions to building dynamic dashboards, I lead projects that turn data into strategic value.

My mission is simple: To connect finance, analytics, and AI, transforming complex data into clarity that drives business growth.

I’m open to collaboration, freelance projects, or full-time roles focused on analytics, finance, or strategy.

My Projects

Estate Financial Tracker

This project analyzes financial transactions within a residential estate to help stakeholders track revenue flow, identify top-paying units, and understand income distribution across multiple bill categories. The dashboard provides an interactive view of revenue trends over time and allows filtering by block, bill type, and month.

Income And Expense Tracker

This project analyzes personal financial data, tracking income, expenses, and savings across categories and time. It helps identify spending patterns, evaluate financial habits, and compare actual expenses against income. The goal is to provide clear, actionable insights into personal budgeting and financial health.

HR Attrition Dashboard

This project analyzes HR data to uncover patterns behind employee attrition and presents actionable insights through an interactive Power BI dashboard.

Student Habits vs Academic Performance

This dashboard project explores the relationship between students' lifestyle habits and their academic performance. By analyzing factors such as study hours, sleep duration, attendance, exercise, diet quality, and mental health, the dashboard reveals insights into how these variables influence exam scores. .

Constrcution Site Inventory Tracker

The Construction Site Inventory Tracker is an Excel-based tool designed to efficiently manage building materials on a construction site. It tracks stock levels, inflow and outflow of materials, and categorizes items for better management. The dashboard provides key performance indicators (KPIs) and visual indicators for stock status, helping teams monitor inventory in real time.

🌍 Global AI Impact Analysis

This project explores how AI adoption impacts industries, workforce, consumer trust, and regulation across countries. Using Python, I cleaned and analyzed a global dataset to uncover trends in AI adoption, job displacement, and market growth. The insights were then transformed into a visually engaging Power BI dashboard, providing a clear overview for stakeholders and policymakers.
