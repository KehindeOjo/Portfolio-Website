Who This Is For

Built for People Starting From Zero

KO DataLab is designed for beginners who want a structured, practical path into Data Analytics, not a theory-heavy course that leaves you with certificates but no confidence.

Complete Beginners

No prior experience with Excel, Power BI, or data needed. We start from the fundamentals and build up.

Career Switchers

Professionals from non-tech backgrounds looking to move into data-related roles with practical, portfolio-ready skills.

Working Professionals

Anyone in finance, admin, or operations who wants to add data skills to their current role and stand out.

Programme Overview

What You'll Learn

Twelve sessions across six weeks, structured to move from data fundamentals to a working Power BI dashboard you can show employers.

Weeks 1–2

Data Analytics Fundamentals

What analysts actually do, how to think about data problems, and the core concepts that everything else builds on.

Weeks 3–4

Excel for Analysis

Formulas, pivot tables, lookups, and building clean, functional spreadsheets using real, Nigerian-context datasets.

Weeks 5–6

Power BI & Dashboards

Data modeling, DAX basics, and building an interactive dashboard you can walk an employer through in an interview.

Why Learn With Me

I Teach What I Use on the Job, Not Just Theory

I am a working Data Analyst, not a full-time trainer running a side hustle. Every dataset and dashboard exercise in KO DataLab comes from the same kind of problems I solve for organizations day to day, financial reporting, audits, and process automation. That is the gap most beginner courses miss: they teach tools in isolation, without showing how analysts actually use them to answer real business questions.
